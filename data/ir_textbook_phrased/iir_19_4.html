the search user experience it is clear that the more user traffic a web-search-engine can attract , the more revenue it stands to earn from sponsored-search . how do search-engines differentiate themselves and grow their traffic ? here google identified two principles that helped it grow at the expense of its competitors : (1) a focus on relevance , specifically precision rather than recall in the first few results ; (2) a user-experience that is lightweight , meaning that both the search-query page and the search-results page are uncluttered and almost entirely textual , with very few graphical-elements . the effect of the first was simply to save users time in locating the information they sought . the effect of the second is to provide a user-experience that is extremely responsive , or at any rate not bottlenecked by the time to load the search-query or results page . subsections user-query needs
