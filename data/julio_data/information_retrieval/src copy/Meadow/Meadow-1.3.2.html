1.3.2 The Database Producer Sequence

The database producer's main goal is to create a database. There are several steps involved in this process. First the decision to create a database must be made. The next step is to determine the scope of the database. This puts some limits on what kind of information will be stored. Next, individual records must be designed. What pieces of information will be stored for each entity. The selection of actual items to be included is done next. Next the content of the individual records is created. The last two steps are data entry and quality control.
