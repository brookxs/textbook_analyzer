8.1 Problems of Query Language Interpretation

Parsing is the process of analyzing a language statement and breaking it into its parts. Problems can arise when parsing due to ambiguity caused by natural language. Many times the context is needed to determine the meaning of a word. The expressiveness of natural language can even mean different meanings when the emphasis is changed from one word to another.
