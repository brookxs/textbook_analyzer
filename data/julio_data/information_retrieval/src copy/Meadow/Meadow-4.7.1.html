4.7.1 Elements of Control

There are four elements that are necessary to control a vocabulary. The first is the vocabulary. It must be a list of terms that is not changed often. The second element is explanatory notes. These are used to help the users choose terms. The most common explanatory notes are dictionaries and thesauri. Communication is the third essential element. The record creators and users must communicate and agree upon the vocabulary. Lastly, there must be a procedure to change the vocabulary.
