4.3 Vector-Based Matching

SMART system is among the earliest successful systems built on the vector model.

In the vector model, similarity measurements can be associated either with the idea of distance called distance measure, that is documents close together in the vector space are highly likely to be similar, or an angular measure, based on the idea that documents "in the same direction" are closely related.

Distance measure, or metrics, can be regarded as dissimilarity measure such that a measure that results in high values represent documents that are dissimilar and low values, such as 0 represents documents that are similar.
