8.4 Average Precision and Recall

Average precision and average recall are the averages of precision and recall that are computed to provide adequate measures by which to judge retrieval systems. There are two methods of calculating the averages:

  1. Three-point averages - averaging the precision of the retrieval system for a given query at three defined recall levels.
  2. Eleven-point averages - averaging the precision of the retrieval system for a given query at eleven defined recall levels.
