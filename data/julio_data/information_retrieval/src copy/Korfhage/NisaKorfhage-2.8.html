2.8 Images and Sound

Images and sounds are becoming more important in the definition of a document and units processing. Multimedia systems (integrated media systems) incorporate sound and animation as well as text and still images. Computer-based retrieval systems that handle images and sound is still in its infancy.

One driving force behind the development of image retrieval systems is the rising interest in geographic information systems. Geographic information systems are systems with data related to a geographic region, such as a city, state, or country.
