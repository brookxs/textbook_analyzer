4.8 Proximity Matching

Proximity matching uses matching method involving the proximity of terms in a text. Modifications of proximity criteria can increase effectiveness. One such modification is to use phrases rather than simple word proximity. Another modification is the use of ordered proximity to aid in the retrieval decision such that the order in which words occur affects their interpretations. Proximity matching can also further be extended by using a measure in the proximity to enhance the search and retrieval of documents.
