7.7 BIRD

BIRD, a Browsing Interface for Retrieval of Documents bases the design of a multiple reference point retrieval system on an array determined by the reference points. The BIRD display consists of two major parts:

  1. Primary portion of the display is a separator array of four cells where documents are distributed among these cells according to their relationship to two chosen reference points.
  2. The second portion of the display consists of a series of classification bin where the user isolates desired documents by moving them to these bins.
