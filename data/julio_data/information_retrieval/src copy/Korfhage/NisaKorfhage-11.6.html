11.6 VIRIs (Visual Information Retrieval Interfaces)

GUIDO, VIBE, and BIRD are three examples of visual information retrieval systems, or VIRIs. Visual interface systems may focus either on presenting the details of an individual document or on presenting the document as a single element within a document collection. Depending on the purpose for which the interface is intended, the objects represented may concentrate on the documents or include elements of a query, classification or retrieval process. Most of the visual interfaces are based on a vector model of he document space and retrieval.
