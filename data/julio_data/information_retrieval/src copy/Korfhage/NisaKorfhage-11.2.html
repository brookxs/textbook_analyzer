11.2 Grouping and Ranking

The organization of the output from a search is important for its proper interpretation. The user will be more satisfied with the effectiveness of the system if the most relevant documents are presented first. Presenting the documents in order of the number of terms matched or in reverse chronological order has some benefit to the user.

In the absence of a ranking function, output from a search can be organized by the principal author because users are more familiar with bibliographic references in documents, which are often organized this way.
