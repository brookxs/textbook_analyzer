5.9 Stemming

Uncontrolled vocabulary presents the problem that each word can occur in different forms. One way to address this issue is by using the stemming algorithm that strips off word endings, reducing them to common core or stem. There are various stemming algorithms. Among them is the iterative approach that requires multiple passes over a given word to get to the stem of the word.

There are two problems in stemming algorithm:

  • The stripping of letter sequences that falsely appears to be endings.
  • Stem changes in various words.
