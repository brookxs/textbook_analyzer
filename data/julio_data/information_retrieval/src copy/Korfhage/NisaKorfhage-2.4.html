2.4 Vocabulary Control

Controlled vocabulary enforces uniformity throughout the retrieval system, making search and retrieval a more efficient process. The controlled vocabulary forces concepts that is similar but slightly different to be treated as one. Thus a query on any one of the related concepts will return all the documents identified by the corresponding controlled vocabulary term.

On the other hand, the uncontrolled vocabulary is the use of uncontrolled indexing in the query matching against documents in the system.
