10.4 Information Filtering and Passage Retrieval

Information filtering or data mining refers to techniques used to quickly eliminate large segments of a database from consideration for a given query. The concept of passage retrieval relates closely to information filtering. Instead of eliminating large portions of a database or many documents, passage retrieval concept identifies within one broad document those passages that relate closely to a given query. Much of the research in passage retrieval is based on differential analysis of the key of terms in a document.
