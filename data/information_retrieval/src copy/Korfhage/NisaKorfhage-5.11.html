5.11 Thesauri

The use of similar or related terms in a retrieval system is another issue to be considered. Thesauri that contain both synonyms and antonyms for each word, together with broader and narrower terms, and closely related terms, are used.

Homographs, two words with distinct meaning but identical spellings presents problems when it is used. Syntactic, semantic, and pragmatic analyses all come into use in determining the equivalences and differences between such words. Homonyms, words that sound alike but have distinct meaning also presents problem.
