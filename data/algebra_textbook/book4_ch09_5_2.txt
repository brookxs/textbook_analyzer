Polygons We can make use of conversion skills with denominate numbers to make measure­ments of geometric figures such as rectangles, triangles, and circles. To make these measurements we need to be familiar with several definitions. Polygon A polygon is a closed plane (flat) figure whose sides are line segments (portions of straight lines).
