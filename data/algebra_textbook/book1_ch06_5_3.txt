Knowing when to Try the Grouping Method We are alerted to the idea of grouping when the polynomial we are considering has either of these qualities: no factor common to all terms an even number of terms When factoring by grouping, the sign ( +  or  − ) of the factor we are taking out will usually (but not always) be the same as the sign of the first term in that group.
