Difference of Two Squares If you see something of the form a2 - b2, you should remember the formula Example:  x2 – 4 = (x – 2)(x + 2) This only holds for a difference of two squares. There is no way to factor a sum of two squares such as a2 + b2 into factors with real numbers.
