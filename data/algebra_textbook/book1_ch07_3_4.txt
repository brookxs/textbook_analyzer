Plotting Points Since points and ordered pairs are so closely related, the two terms are sometimes used interchangeably. The following two phrases have the same meaning: Plot the point (a, b) . Plot the ordered pair (a, b) . Plotting a Point Both phrases mean: Locate, in the plane, the point associated with the ordered pair (a, b) and draw a mark at that position.
