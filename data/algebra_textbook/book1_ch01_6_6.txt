Addition and Subtraction of Fractions Fractions with Like Denominators To add (or subtract) two or more fractions that have the same denominators, add (or subtract) the numerators and place the resulting sum over the common denominator. Reduce if possible. CAUTIONAdd or subtract only the numerators. Do not add or subtract the denominators!
