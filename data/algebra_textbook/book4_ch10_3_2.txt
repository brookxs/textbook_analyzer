Positive and Negative Numbers Positive and Negative Numbers Each real number other than zero has a sign associated with it. A real number is said to be a positive number if it is to the right of 0 on the number line and negative if it is to the left of 0 on the number line. THE NOTATION OF SIGNED NUMBERS + and – Notation A number is denoted as positive if it is directly preceded by a plus sign or no sign at all.
