The Power Rule for Quotients The following example suggests a rule for raising a quotient to a power. Example 2.112.  POWER RULE FOR QUOTIENTS If x and y are real numbers and n is a natural number, To raise a quotient to a power, distribute the exponent to both the numerator and denominator.
