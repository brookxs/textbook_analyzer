Multiplying a Polynomial by a Monomial Multiplying a polynomial by a monomial is a direct application of the distributive property. Distributive Property The distributive property suggests the following rule. Multiplying a Polynomial by a Monomial To multiply a polynomial by a monomial, multiply every term of the polynomial by the monomial and then add the resulting products together.
