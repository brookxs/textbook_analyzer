The Process We can now state a process for reducing a rational expression. Reducing a Rational Expression Factor the numerator and denominator completely. Divide the numerator and denominator by all factors they have in common, that is, remove all factors of 1. Reduced to Lowest Terms A rational expression is said to be reduced to lowest terms when the numerator and denominator have no factors in common.
