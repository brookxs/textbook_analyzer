tweetmogaz : a news-portal of tweets twitter is currently one of the largest social hubs for users to spread and discuss news . for most of the top news stories happening , there are corresponding discussions on social-media . in this demonstration tweetmogaz is presented , which is a platform for microblog search and filtering . it creates a real-time comprehensive report about what people discuss and share around news happening in certain regions . tweetmogaz reports the most popular tweets , jokes , videos , images , and news-articles that people share about top news stories . moreover , it allows users to search for specific topics . a scalable automatic technique for microblog filtering is used to obtain relevant tweets to a certain news category in a region . tweetmogaz.com demonstrates the effectiveness of our filtering technique for reporting public response toward news in different arabic regions including egypt and syria in real-time .
