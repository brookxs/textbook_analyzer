messidor : a distributed-information-retrieval system messidor is an interactive-retrieval system . it differs from current systems in that it allows the <u> simultaneous search of several bibliographic-databases </u> . the databases may be on different sites such as esa in frascati , telesystemes in sophia antipolis ... the databases may use different query-languages such as quest , mistral ... however a user converses with the system messidor with <u> a single language </u> , the messidor language.the system 's prototype is implemented on a micral 80 -- 30 micro-computer .
