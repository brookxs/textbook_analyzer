development of the bds online-information retrieval-system beijing document service (bds) is a new-type information-service providing online-information retrieval service mainly in beijing and other cities besides . this paper presents the background , goals , design-considerations of the bds system and what have been done in achieving the goal of loading the ntis bibliographic-data base into the system and providing its online service to the public . system appraisal is covered and in light of problems experienced by bds , possible approaches of how modernized information-retrieval-systems might be developed in china are also discussed .
