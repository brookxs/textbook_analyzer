text-collections for fire the aim of the forum for information-retrieval-evaluation (fire) is to create a cranfield-like evaluation-framework in the spirit of trec , clef and ntcir , for indian-language-information-retrieval . for the first year , six indian-languages have been selected : bengali , hindi , marathi , punjabi , tamil , and telugu . this poster describes the tasks as well as the document and topic collections that are to be used at the fire workshop .
