relevance-criteria for e-commerce : a crowdsourcing-based experimental-analysis we discuss the concept of relevance-criteria in the context of e-commerce search . a vast body of research-literature describes the beyond-topical criteria used to determine the relevance of the document to the need . we argue that in an e-commerce scenario there are some differences , and novel and different criteria can be used to determine relevance . we experimentally validate this hypothesis by means of amazon-mechanical-turk using a crowdsourcing approach .
