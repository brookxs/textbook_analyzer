file organizations & incrementally specified queries queries to information-retrieval-systems are often incrementally specified as a result of user-interaction with the system . however , most discussions of file organizations consider only completely specified queries . the choice of file organizations to support such incremental-specification is discussed qualitatively in this extended abstract . (quantitative comparisons are partially complete and are not presented here .) organizations which are advantageous for completely specified queries are not necessarily so for incrementally specified queries (and vice versa) .
