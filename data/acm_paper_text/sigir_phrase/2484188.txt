entity-linking and retrieval this full-day tutorial presents a comprehensive introduction to entity-linking and retrieval . part i provides a detailed overview of entity-linking : identifying and disambiguating entity occurrences in unstructured-text . part ii focuses on entity-retrieval , by first considering scenarios where explicit representations of entities are available , and then moving to a setting where evidence needs to be collected and aggregated from multiple-documents or even collections , thereby combining techniques from both entity-linking and entity-retrieval . part iii concludes the tutorial with an overview and hands-on comparative-analysis of applications and publicly available toolkits and web-services .
