sensor-aided mobile-information management and retrieval the number of `` smart '' mobile-devices such as wireless phones and tablet-computers has been rapidly growing . these mobile-devices are equipped with a variety of sensors such as camera , gyroscope , accelerometer , compass , nfc , wifi , gps , etc. . these sensors can be used to capture images and voice , detect motion-patterns , and predict locations , to name just a few . this keynote depicts techniques in configuration , calibration , computation , and fusion for improving sensor performance and conserving power-consumption . we also present novel mobile-information management and retrieval applications that can benefit a great deal from enhanced sensor-technologies .
