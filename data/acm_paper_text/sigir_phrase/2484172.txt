ranking explanatory sentences for opinion-summarization we introduce a novel sentence-ranking problem called explanatory-sentence extraction (ese) which aims to rank sentences in opinionated text based on their usefulness for helping users understand the detailed reasons of sentiments (i.e. , `` explanatoriness '') . we propose and study several general methods for scoring the explanatoriness of a sentence . we create new data-sets and propose a new measure for evaluation . experiment results show that the proposed methods are effective , outperforming a state-of-the-art sentence-ranking method for standard text-summarization .
