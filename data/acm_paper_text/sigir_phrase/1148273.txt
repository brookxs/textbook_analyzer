clustering of search-results using temporal attributes clustering of search-results is an important feature in many of today 's information-retrieval applications . the notion of hit-list-clustering appears in web-search-engines and enterprise-search engines as a mechanism that allows users to further explore the coverage of a query . however , there has been little work on exposing temporal attributes for constructing and presentation of clusters . these attributes appear in documents as part of the textual content , e.g. , as a date and time token or as a temporal-reference in a sentence . in this paper , we outline a model and describe a prototype that shows the main ideas .
