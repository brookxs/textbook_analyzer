focused ranking in a vertical-search-engine since the debut of pagerank and hits , hyperlink-induced web-document ranking has come a long way . the-web has become increasingly vast and topically diverse . such vastness has led many into the area of topic-sensitive-ranking and its variants . we address the high-dimensionality of the-web by providing tools for focused-search . a focused-search engine is one which seeks coverage over a subset of topics of the-web and presents users with relevant search-results in a known domain . this demonstration will introduce readers to the genieknows.com vertical-search-engine .
