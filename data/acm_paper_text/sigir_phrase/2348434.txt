mydj : recommending karaoke songs from one 's own voice in this demo , we present mydj , a karaoke recommendation-system which recommends the songs people are capable to sing . different from the existing song recommendation-systems which recommend songs people like to listen , mydj can recommend proper songs according to a subject 's physical phonation area . it consists of a singer profiler to analyze the subject 's phonation characters . in addition , the song profile for each song in database is extracted . to learn a ranking-function , the learning-to-rank algorithm listnet is applied under a list of predefined features extracted from each singer-song profile pair . in the results , proper songs which are suitable but challenging for the subject are recommended .
