a method to automatically construct a user-knowledge model in a forum environment having a mechanism to validate the opinions and to identify experts in a forum could help people to favor one opinion against another . to achieve this , some solutions have already been introduced , including social-network-analysis techniques and reputation modeling . however , neither of these solutions considers the users ' knowledge to identify an expert . in this paper , a novel method is proposed which estimates users ' knowledge based on the forum itself , and identifies the possible areas of expertise associated with each user .
