feature-engineering for mobile (sms) spam-filtering mobile-spam in an increasing threat that may be addressed using filtering systems like those employed against email-spam . we believe that email-filtering techniques require some adaptation to reach good levels of performance on sms-spam , especially regarding message representation . in order to test this assumption , we have performed experiments on sms filtering using top performing email-spam filters on mobile-spam messages using a suitable feature-representation , with results supporting our hypothesis .
