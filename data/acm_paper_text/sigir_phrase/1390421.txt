automatically identifying localizable queries personalization of web-search-results as a technique for improving user-satisfaction has received notable attention in the research-community over the past decade . much of this work focuses on modeling and establishing a profile for each user to aid in personalization . our work takes a more query-centric approach . in this paper , we present a method for efficient , automatic-identification of a class of queries we define as localizable from a web-search-engine query-log . we determine a set of relevant features and use conventional machine-learning-techniques to classify queries . our experiments find that our technique is able to identify localizable queries with 94 \ % accuracy .
