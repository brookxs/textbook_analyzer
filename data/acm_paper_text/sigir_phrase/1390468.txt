towards personalized distributed-information-retrieval our aim is to investigate if and how the performance of distributed-information-retrieval (dir) systems can be improved through personalization . toward this aim we are building a testbed of document-collections and corresponding personalized relevance-judgments . in this paper we discuss our intended approach for personalizing the three different phases of the dir process . we also describe the test-collection we are building and discuss our methodology for evaluating personalized dir using relevance information taken from social-bookmarking data .
