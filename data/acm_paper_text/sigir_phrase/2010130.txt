how to count thumb-ups and thumb-downs ? : an information-retrieval approach to user-rating based ranking of items it is a common practice among web-2.0-services to allow users to rate items on their sites . in this paper , we first point out the flaws of the popular methods for user-rating based ranking of items , and then argue that two well-known information-retrieval (ir) techniques , namely the-probability-ranking-principle and statistical-language-modelling , provide a simple but effective solution to this problem .
