retrieval based on user-behaviour this paper gives an overview of the ongoing research in the active data bases project at the vrije universiteit , amsterdam . in this project we are specifying and building a system that helps a user in his search for useful and interesting information in large , complex-information-systems . the system is able to do this , because it learns from the interaction about the users and the data it contains . the indications of the users are expressed in terms of interests in the data , which serve as building-blocks for user and data-models . these models are then used to improve the search for interesting data .
