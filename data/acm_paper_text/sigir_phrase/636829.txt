mars : a retrieval-tool on the basis of morphological-analysis in this paper we present the system mars which has been designed for potential database users working with retrieval-systems . it is an instrument that is to assist the retriever in specifying the search queries . mars is an aid for increasing termprecision as well as for finding previously unknown terms which are morphologically related to user-search terms . this is achieved by way of linguistic procedures as opposed to conventional mechanical operations such as truncation etc. . the morpheme lexicon and the morphological-decomposition procedures are described briefly . mars was put to the test in a real-world situation the results of which will be presented .
