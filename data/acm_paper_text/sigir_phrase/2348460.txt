collaborative-filtering with short term preferences mining recently , recommender-systems have fascinated researchers and benefited a variety of people 's online activities , enabling users to survive the explosive web-information . traditional collaborative-filtering techniques handle the general recommendation well . however , most such approaches usually focus on long-term preferences . to discover more short term factors influencing people 's decisions , we propose a short term preferences model , implemented with implicit-user-feedback . we conduct experiments comparing the performances of different short term models , which show that our model outperforms significantly compared to those long-term models .
