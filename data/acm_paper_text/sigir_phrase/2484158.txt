who will retweet me ? : finding retweeters in twitter an important aspect of communication in twitter (and other social-network is message propagation -- people creating posts for others to share . although there has been work on modelling how tweets in twitter are propagated (retweeted) , an untackled problem has been who will retweet a message . here we consider the task of finding who will retweet a message posted on twitter . within a learning to-rank framework , we explore a wide range of features , such as retweet history , followers status , followers active time and followers interests . we find that followers who retweeted or mentioned the author 's tweets frequently before and have common interests are more likely to be retweeters .
