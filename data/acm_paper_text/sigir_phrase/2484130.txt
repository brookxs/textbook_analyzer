using social-annotations to enhance document-representation for personalized-search in this paper , we present a contribution to ir modeling . we propose an approach that computes on-the-fly , a personalized social-document representation (psdr) of each document per user based on his social-activities . the psdrs are used to rank documents with respect to a query . this approach has been intensively evaluated on a large public dataset , showing significant benefits for personalized-search .
