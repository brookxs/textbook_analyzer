product retrieval for grocery stores we introduce a grocery-retrieval system that maps shopping-lists written in natural-language into actual products in a grocery store . we have developed the system using nine months of shopping basket data from a large finnish supermarket . to evaluate the system , we used 70 real shopping-lists gathered from customers of the supermarket . our system achieves over 80 \ % precision for products at rank-one , and the precision is around 70 \ % for products at rank 5 .
