mobile-interface of the memoria project this project develops tools to manage personal-memories that include a multimedia-retrieval system and user-interfaces for different devices . this paper and demonstration presents the mobile-interface which allows browsing , retrieving , and taking pictures that are automatically annotated with gps-data and audio information . the multimedia-retrieval system uses multimodal-information : visual-content , gps metadata and audio information . the interface was evaluated in a cultural-heritage site .
