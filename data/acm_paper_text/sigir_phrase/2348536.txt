patent information-retrieval : an instance of domain-specific-search the tutorial aims to provide the ir researchers with an understanding of how the patent system works , the challenges that patent searchers face in using the existing tools and in adopting new methods developed in academia . at the same time , the tutorial will inform the ir researcher about the unique opportunities that the patent domain provides : a large amount of multi-lingual and multi-modal documents , the widest possible span of covered domains , a highly annotated-corpus and , very importantly , relevance-judgements created by experts in the fields and recorded electronically in the documents . the combination of these two objectives leads to the main purpose of the tutorial : to create awareness and to encourage more emphasis on the patent domain in the ir community . table 1 provides details on how the tutorial covers the topics of the sigir conference .
