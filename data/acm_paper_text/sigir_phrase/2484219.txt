a geolinguistic web-application based on linked-open-data digital geolinguistic systems encourage collaboration between linguists , historians , archaeologists , ethnographers , as they explore the relationship between language and cultural adaptation and change . in this demo , we propose a linked-open-data approach for increasing the level of interoperability of geolinguistic applications and the reuse of the data . we present a case-study of a geolinguistic project named atlante sintattico d'italia , syntactic atlas of italy (asit) .
