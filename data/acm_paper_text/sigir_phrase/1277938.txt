hits on question answer portals : exploration of link-analysis for author-ranking question-answer-portals such as naver and yahoo!-answers are growing in popularity . however , despite the increased popularity , the quality of answers is uneven , and while some users usually provide good answers , many others often provide bad answers . hence , estimating the authority , or the expected quality of users , is a crucial task for this emerging domain , with potential applications to answer-ranking and to incentive-mechanism design . we adapt a powerful link-analysis methodology from the-web domain as a first step towards estimating authority in question answer portals . our experimental-results over more than 3 million answers from yahoo!-answers are promising , and warrant further exploration along the lines outlined in this poster .
