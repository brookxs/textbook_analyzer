query-rewriting using active-learning for sponsored-search sponsored-search is a major revenue source for search companies . web searchers can issue any queries , while advertisement keywords are limited . query-rewriting-technique effectively matches user queries with relevant advertisement keywords , thus increases the amount of web advertisements available . the match relevance is critical for clicks . in this study , we aim to improve query-rewriting relevance . for this purpose , we use an active-learning-algorithm called transductive-experimental-design to select the most informative samples to train the query-rewriting relevance-model . experiments show that this approach significantly improves model-accuracy and rewriting relevance .
