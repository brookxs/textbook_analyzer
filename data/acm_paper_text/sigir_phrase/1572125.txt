serendipitous search via wikipedia : a query-log-analysis we analyse the query-log of a click-oriented japanese search-engine that utilises the link-structures of wikipedia for encouraging the user to change his information-need and to perform repeated , serendipitous , exploratory-search . our results show that users tend to make transitions within the same query type : from person-names to person-names , from place names to place names , and so on .
