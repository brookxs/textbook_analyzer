relevance-judgments between trec and non-trec assessors this paper investigates the agreement of relevance-assessments between official trec judgments and those generated from an interactive-ir experiment . results show that 63 \ % of documents judged relevant by our users matched official trec judgments . several factors contributed to differences in the agreements : the number of retrieved relevant documents ; the number of relevant documents judged ; system effectiveness per topic and the ranking of relevant documents .
