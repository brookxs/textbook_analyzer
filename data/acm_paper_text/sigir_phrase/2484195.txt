searching in the city of knowledge : challenges and recent developments today plenty of data is emerging from various city systems . beyond the classical web-resources , large amounts of data are retrieved from sensors , devices , social-networks , governmental applications , or service-networks . in such a diversity-of-information , answering specific information-needs of city inhabitants requires holistic ir techniques , capable of harnessing different types of city data and turned it into actionable insights to answer different queries . this tutorial will present deep insights , challenges , opportunities and techniques to make heterogeneous city data searchable and show how emerging ir techniques models can be employed to retrieve relevant-information for the citizens .
