summarizing the differences from microblogs with the rapid growth of social-media websites , microblogging has become a popular way to spread instant news and events . due to the dynamic and social nature of microblogs , extracting useful information from microblogs is more challenging than from the traditional news-articles . in this paper we study the problem of summarizing the differences from microblogs . given a collection of microblogs discussing an event/topic , we propose to generate a short summary delivering the differences among these microblogs , such as the different points of view for a news topic and the changes and evolution of an ongoing event .
