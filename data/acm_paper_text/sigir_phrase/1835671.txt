learning the click-through-rate for rare/new ads from similar ads ads on the search-engine (se) are generally ranked based on their click-through-rates (ctr) . hence , accurately predicting the ctr of an ad is of paramount importance for maximizing the se 's revenue . we present a model that inherits the click information of rare/new ads from other semantically related ads . the semantic features are derived from the query ad click-through graphs and advertisers account information . we show that the model learned using these features give a very good prediction for the ctr values .
