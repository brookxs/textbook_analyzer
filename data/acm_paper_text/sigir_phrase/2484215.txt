themestreams : visualizing the stream of themes discussed in politics the political landscape is fluid . discussions are always ongoing and new `` hot-topics '' continue to appear in the headlines . but what made people start talking about that topic ? and who started it ? because of the speed at which discussions sometimes take place this can be difficult to track down . we describe themestreams : a demonstrator that maps political discussions to themes and influencers and illustrate how this mapping is used in an interactive-visualization that shows us which themes are being discussed , and that helps us answer the question `` who put this issue on the map ? '' in streams of political-data .
