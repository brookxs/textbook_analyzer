a multilingual and multiplatform application for medicinal plants prescription from medical symptoms this paper presents an application for medicinal plants prescription based on text-classification-techniques . the system receives as an input a free-text describing the symptoms of a user , and retrieves a ranked list of medicinal plants related to those symptoms . in addition , a set of links to wikipedia are also provided , enriching the information about every medicinal plant presented to the user . in order to improve the accessibility to the application , the input can be written in six different languages , adapting the results accordingly . the application-interface can be accessed from different devices and platforms .
