accommodating colorblind users in image-search there are about 8 \ % of men and 0.8 \ % of women suffering from colorblindness . due to certain loss of color information , the existing image-search techniques may not provide satisfactory results for these users . in this demonstration , we show an image-search system that can accommodate colorblind users . it can help these special users find and enjoy what they want by providing multiple-services for them , including search-results reranking , image recoloring and color indication .
