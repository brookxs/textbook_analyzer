cue : a preprocessor system for restricted , natural english cue , an input-interface system which permits the computer to utilize natural but restricted english as input , is presented . in addition , an experimental model for cue , proto-relades , which can `` understand '' and execute english sentences about the content of the library at ibm 's boston programming center is described . these sentences can be query , command , or conditional sentences . the linguistic component of the system is based on a transformational-grammar of english that performs a full syntactic-and-semantic-analysis of each input sentence and translates it into relevant computer-operations . the capabilities and limitations of this system are described .
