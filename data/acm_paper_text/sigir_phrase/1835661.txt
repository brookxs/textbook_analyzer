a two-stage-model for blog-feed-search we consider blog-feed-search : identifying relevant blogs for a given topic . an individual 's search-behavior often involves a combination of exploratory-behavior triggered by salient-features of the information objects being examined plus goal-directed in-depth information-seeking-behavior . we present a two-stage blog-feed-search model that directly builds on this insight . we first rank blog posts for a given topic , and use their parent blogs as selection of blogs that we rank using a blog-based model .
