on diversifying and personalizing web-search diversification and personalization methods are common ap-proaches to deal with the one-size-fits-all paradigm of web-search-engines . we performed a user-study with 190 subjects where we analyzed the effects of diversification and personalization methods in a web-search-engine . the obtained results suggest that our proposed combination of diversification and personalization factors may be a way to overcome the notion of intrusiveness in personalized approaches .
