a review of relevance-feedback experiments at the 2003 reliable information-access (ria) workshop . we review here the results of one of the experiments performed at the 2003 reliable information-access (ria) workshop , hosted by mitre corporation and the northeast regional research center (nrrc) . the experiment concentrates on query-expansion using relevance-feedback and explores the behaviour of several information-retrieval-systems using variable numbers of relevant documents .
