opinion-holder extraction from author and authority viewpoints opinion-holder extraction research is important for discriminating between opinions that are viewed from different perspectives . in this paper , we describe our experience of participation in the ntcir-6 opinion-analysis pilot task by focusing on opinion-holder extraction results in japanese and english . our approach to opinion-holder extraction was based on the discrimination between author and authority viewpoints in opinionated sentences , and the evaluation results were fair with respect to the japanese documents .
