designing search-usability search is not just a box and ten blue links . search is a journey : an exploration where what we encounter along the way changes what we seek . but in order to guide people along this journey , we must understand both the art and science of search experience-design . the aim of this tutorial is to deliver a course grounded in good scholarship , integrating the latest research findings with insights derived from the practical-experience of designing and optimizing an extensive range of commercial search applications . it focuses on the development of transferable , practical skills that can be learnt and practiced within a half-day session .
