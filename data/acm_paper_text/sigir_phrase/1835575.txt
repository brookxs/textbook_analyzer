multilingual people-search people-search is an important search-service with multiple-applications (eg . looking up a friend on facebook , finding colleagues in corporate email directories etc) . with the proportion of non-english users on a steady rise , people-search services are being used by users from diverse language demographics . users may issue name-search queries against these directories in languages other than the language of the directory , in which case the present monolingual name-search approaches will not work . in this demo , we present a multilingual people-search system capable of performing fast name lookups on large user directories , independent of the directory language . our system has applications in areas like social-networking , enterprise-search and email-address book-search .
