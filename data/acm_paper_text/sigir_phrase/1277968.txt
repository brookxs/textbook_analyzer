x-site : a workplace search-tool for software-engineers professionals in the workplace need high-precision search-tools capable of retrieving information that is useful and appropriate to the task at hand . one approach to identifying content , which is not only relevant but also useful , is to make use of the task-context of the search . we present x-site , an enterprise-search engine for the software-engineering domain that exploits relationships between user 's tasks and document genres in the collection to improve retrieval-precision .
