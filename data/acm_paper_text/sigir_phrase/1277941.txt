an effective method for finding best-entry-points in semi-structured-documents focused structured-document-retrieval employs the concept of best-entry-point (bep) , which is intended to provide optimal starting-point from which users can browse to relevant-document components [4] . in this paper we describe and evaluate a method for finding beps in xml-documents . experiments conducted within the framework of inex 2006 evaluation campaign on the wikipedia xml collection [2] shown the effectiveness of the proposed approach .
