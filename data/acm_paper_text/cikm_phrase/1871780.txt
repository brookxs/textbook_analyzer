falcon : seamless access to meeting data from the inbox and calendar we present a system that supports seamless access-to-information contained in recorded meetings from the cornerstone points of a knowledge-worker 's daily-life : mailbox and calendar . the solution supports granular search of meeting content from an enterprise email system and automatically displays recordings of meetings related to the message the user is currently viewing . additionally thumbnail summaries of the meetings are added to the user 's calendar entries after the meetings have taken place . lastly our system supports easy sharing of videos associated with recorded meetings through the use of hot-linked thumbnail summaries which can be sent via email .
