pikm 2010 : acm workshop for ph.d. students in information-and-knowledge-management the pikm workshop focuses on papers consisting mainly of the ph.d. dissertation proposals of doctoral students . a wide range of topics on any area in databases , information-retrieval and knowledge-management are presented at this workshop . the areas-of-interest are similar to those at the cikm main conference in the three respective tracks . interdisciplinary-work across these tracks is encouraged .
