dtmbio 2011 : international workshop on data and textmining in biomedical-informatics acm fifth international workshop on data and text-mining in biomedical-informatics (dtmbio 11) organizers are pleased to announce that the fifth dtmbio will be held in conjunction with cikm , one of the largest data and text-mining conferences . while cikm presents the state-of-the-art research in informatics with the primary focus on data and text-mining , the main focus of dtmbio is on biomedical-informatics . dtmbio delegates will bring forth interesting applications of up-to-date informatics in the context of biomedical-research .
