lsds-ir ' 11 : the 9th workshop on large-scale and distributed-systems for information-retrieval the growth of the-web and user bases lead to important performance-problems for large-scale web search-engines . the lsds - ir '11 workshop focuses on research contributions related to the scalability and efficiency of distributed-information-retrieval (ir) systems . the workshop also encourages contributions that propose different ways of leveraging diversity and multiplicity of resources available in distributed-systems . more specifically , we are interested in novel applications , models , and architectures that deal with efficiency and scalability of distributed-ir systems .
