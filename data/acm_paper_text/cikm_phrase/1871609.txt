on top-k social-web-search to enhance the quality of document-search , recent research studies have started to exploit the social-networks of users by considering social-influence (si) , measurement of the affinity between a query user and the publisher of a retrieved document , in addition to the commonly used textual relevance (tr) . we refer to such document-search that considers social-networks as social-web-search . in this paper , we focus on efficient top - k social-web-search and propose two search-strategies : (i) tr-based search and (ii) si-based search that tailor document examination orders upon tr and si , respectively . we evaluate the proposed strategies through experimentation .
