fu-finder : a game for studying querying behaviours usually the focus of evaluation within information-retrieval has been placed largely upon the system . however , the individual user and their submitted queries are typically the greatest source of variation in the search-process . this demonstration paper presents fu-finder , a fun and enjoyable game that measures the user 's querying abilities (or search-fu) . this game provides useful data for the study of user querying-behaviour and assesses how well users can find specific web-pages using different search-engines .
