pdfmeat : managing publications on the semantic-desktop researchers maintain bibliographies and extensive sets of pdf-files of scholarly-publications on their desktop . the lack of proper metadata of downloaded pdfs makes this task a tedious one . with pdfmeat we present a solution to automatically determine publication metadata for scholarly papers within the user 's desktop-environment and link the metadata to the files . pdfmeat effectively matches local full texts to an online repository . in an evaluation for more than 2.000 diverse pdf-files it worked highly reliable and showed excellent accuracy of up to 98 percent . we demonstrate pdfmeat for different sets of papers , highlighting the semantic integration and use of the retrieved metadata within the file browser of the desktop-environment .
