entityengine : answering entity-relationship queries using shallow semantics we introduce entityengine , a system for answering entity-relationship queries over text . such queries combine sql-like structures with ir-style keyword constraints and therefore , can be expressive and flexible in querying about entities and their relationships . entityengine consists of various offline and online components , including a position-based ranking-model for accurate ranking of query answers and a novel entity-centric index for efficient-query-evaluation .
