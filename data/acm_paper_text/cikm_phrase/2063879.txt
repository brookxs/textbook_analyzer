recommending citations with translation-model citation-recommendation is useful for an author to find out the papers or books that can support the materials she is writing about . it is a challengeable problem since the vocabulary used in the content of papers and in the citation-contexts are usually quite different . to address this problem , we propose to use translation-model , which can bridge the gap between two heterogeneous languages . we conduct an experiment and find the translation-model can provide much better candidates of citations than the state-of-the-art methods .
