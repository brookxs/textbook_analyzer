comments-oriented blog summarization by sentence-extraction much existing research on blogs focused on posts only , ignoring their comments . our user-study conducted on summarizing blog posts , however , showed that reading comments does change one 's understanding about blog posts . in this research , we aim to extract representative sentences from a blog post that best represent the topics discussed among its comments . the proposed solution first derives representative words from comments and then selects sentences containing representative words . the representativeness of words is measured using requt (i.e. , re ader , qu otation , and t opic) . evaluated on human labeled sentences , requt together with summation-based sentence-selection showed promising results .
