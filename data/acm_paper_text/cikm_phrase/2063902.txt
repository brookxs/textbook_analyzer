beyond relevance in marketplace search in this paper we study diversity and its relations to search-relevance in the context of an online marketplace . we conduct a large-scale log-based study using click-stream data from a leading ecommerce site . we introduce 3 main metrics -- selection (diversity) , trust , and value . in our analysis we also show how these interact with relevance in different ways . we study the benefits of diversity and also show why guaranteeing diversity is important .
