widm 2012 : the 12th international workshop on web-information and data-management we give an overview of widm 2012 , held in conjunction with cikm 2012 in maui , hawaii . widm 2012 is the twelfth in a series of international-workshops on web-information and data-management held in conjunction with cikm since 1998 . the objective of the workshop is to bring together researchers and industrial practitioners to present and discuss leading research into how web-data and information can be extracted , stored , analyzed , and processed to provide useful knowledge to end-users for advanced database and web-applications .
