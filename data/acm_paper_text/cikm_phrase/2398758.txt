dtmbio 2012 : international workshop on data and text-mining in biomedical-informatics the organizers of acm sixth international workshop on data and text-mining in biomedical-informatics (dtmbio 12) are happy announce that the sixth dtmbio will be held in conjunction with cikm , one of the largest data-management conferences . the major interests of dtmbio are on the state-of-the-art applications of data and text-mining on biomedical-research problems . dtmbio 12 will be a forum of discussing and exchanging informatics related techniques and problems in the context of biomedical-research .
