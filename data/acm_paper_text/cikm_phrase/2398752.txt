clouddb 2012 : fourth international workshop on cloud-data-management the fourth acm international workshop on cloud-data-management is held in maui , hawaii , usa on october 29 , 2012 and co-located with the acm 21th conference on information-and-knowledge-management (cikm) . the main objective of the workshop is to address the challenges of large-scale-data management based on the cloud-computing infrastructure . the workshop brings together researchers and practitioners from cloud-computing , distributed-storage , query-processing , parallel-algorithms , data-mining , and system-analysis , all attendees share common research interests in maximizing performance , reducing cost of cloud-data-management and enlarging the scale of their endeavors . we have constructed an exciting program of seven refereed papers and four invited keynote talks that will give participants a full dose of emerging research .
