technical architecture overview : tools for acquisition , packaging and ingest of web-objects into multiple repositories this poster describes a model for acquiring , packaging and ingesting web-objects for archiving in multiple repositories . this ongoing work is part of the echo depository project , a 3-year ndiipp-partner digital-preservation project at the university of illinois at urbana-champaign with partners oclc , a consortium of content provider partners , and the library-of-congress .
