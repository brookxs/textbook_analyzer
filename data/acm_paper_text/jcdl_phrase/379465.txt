iscapes : digital-libraries environments for the promotion of scientific-thinking by undergraduates in geography this paper reviews considerations associated with implementing the ale xandria digital earth prototype (adept) in undergraduate geography education by means of iscapes (or information-landscapes) . in particular , we are interested in how iscapes might be used to promote scientific-thinking by undergraduate-students . based upon an ongoing educational needs-assessment , we present a set of conceptual principles that might selectively be implemented in the design of educational-digital-library environments .
