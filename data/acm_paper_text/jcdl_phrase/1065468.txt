pdlib : personal digital libraries with universal-access we propose a universally available personal-digital-library system . it is `` personal '' in the sense that each user is provided with a general purpose document-repository (i.e. a personal-digital-library) . it is `` universally available '' in the sense that it allows the user to access her/his personal personal-digital-library from most computing devices connected to the-internet , including mobile-phones , pdas and laptops , therefore granting access `` from anyplace at anytime .
