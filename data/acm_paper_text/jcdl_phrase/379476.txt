comprehensive access to printed materials (capm) the capm project features the development and evaluation of an automat ed , robotic on-demand scanning system for materials at remote locations . to date , we have developed a book-retrieval robot and a valuation analysis framework for evaluating capm . we intend to augment capm by exploring approaches for automated page turning and improved valuation . these extensions will results in a more fully automated capm system and a valuation framework that will not only be useful for assessing capm specifically , but also for library-services and functions generally .
