curriculum-development for digital-libraries the virginia tech department of computer-science (vt cs) and the university of north carolina at chapel hill school of information and library-science (unc sils) have launched a curriculum-development project in the area of digital-libraries . educational-resources will be developed based on the acm/ieee-cs computing-curriculum 2001 . lesson-plans and modules will be developed in a variety of areas (that cover the topics of papers and conference sessions in the field) , evaluated by experts in those areas , and then pilot tested in cs and lis courses . an analysis of papers on digital library-related topics from several corpora was performed , to identify the areas in which more and less work has already been performed on these topics ; this analysis will guide the initial stages of this curriculum-development .
