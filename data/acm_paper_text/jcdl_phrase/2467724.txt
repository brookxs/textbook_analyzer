tipple : location-triggered mobile-access to a-digital library for audio-books this paper explores the role of audio as a means to access books in a-digital library while being at the location referred to in the books . the books are sourced from the digital library and can either be accompanied by pre-recorded audio or synthesized using text-to-speech . the paper details the functional-requirements , design-and-implementation of tipple . the concept was extensively tested in three field-studies .
