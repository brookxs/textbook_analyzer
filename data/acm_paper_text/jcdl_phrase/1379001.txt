caspar : cultural , artistic and scientific-knowledge for preservation , access and retrieval caspar (cultural , artistic , and scientific-knowledge for preservation , access , and retrieval) aims at providing secure , reliable and cost-effective access for digitally encoded information for an indefinite time frame . from digital pictures to museum archives , from music to scientific-data sets , technology is constantly changing . caspar is being developed to address this situation , and provide people and organisations with a user-oriented preservation framework .
