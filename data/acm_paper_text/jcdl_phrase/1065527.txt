copyright transfer agreements and self-archiving concerns about intellectual-property-rights are a significant barrier to the practice of scholarly self-archiving in institutional and other types of digital-repositories . this introductory level , half-day tutorial will demystify the journal copyright transfer agreements (ctas) that often are the source of these rights concerns of scholars . in addition , participants will be introduced to the deposit processes of self-archiving in an interdisciplinary repository and open-access archive (oaa) , such as dlist , digital-library for information-science and technology .
