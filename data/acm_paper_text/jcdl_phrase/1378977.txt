the vocalsearch music-search-engine the vocalsearch system is a music-search-engine developed at northwestern university and available on the internet (vocalsearch.org) . this system lets the user-query for the desired song in a number of ways : sung queries , queries entered as music-notation , and text-based lyrics search . users are also able to contribute songs to the system , making them searchable for future users . the result is a flexible system that lets the user find the song using their preferred modality (music-notation , text , music-notation) . this demonstration lets users try out the vocalsearch system .
