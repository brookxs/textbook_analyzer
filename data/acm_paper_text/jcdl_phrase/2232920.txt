yadda2 : assemble your own digital-library application from lego-bricks yadda2 is an open-software platform which facilitates creation of digital-library-applications . it consists of versatile building-blocks providing , among others : storage , relational and full-text-indexing , process-management , and asynchronous-communication . its loosely-coupled service-oriented-architecture enables deployment of highly-scalable , distributed-systems .
