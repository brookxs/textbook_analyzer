digital-library-education in computer-science programs in an effort to identify the `` state-of-the-art '' in digital-library-education in computer-science (cs) programs , we analyzed cs courses on digital-libraries and digital library-related topics . fifteen courses that mention digital-libraries in the title or short description were identified ; of these , five are concerned with digital-libraries as the primary topic of the course . the readings from these five courses were analyzed further , in terms of their authors and the journals in which they were published .
