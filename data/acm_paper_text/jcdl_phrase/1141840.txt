feasibility of developing curriculum standards metadata we asked 18 teachers about their use-of-video and digital-video in the classroom . in general teachers desired digital-collections organized by curriculum objectives because curriculum objectives allowed them to quickly narrow search-results based on particular units and objectives . to understand the implications for creating metadata , an example video was tagged according to 3 different state curricular standards . the time intensive task required both in-depth knowledge of the video and of the standards . this finding has implications for third party metadata-generation .
