treating data like software : a case for production quality-data in this short paper , we describe the production data approach to data-curation . we argue that by treating data in a similar fashion to how we build production software , that data will be more readily accessible and available for broad re-use . we should be treating data as an ongoing process . this includes considering third-party contributions ; planning for cyclical releases ; bug-fixes , tracking , and versioning ; and issuing licensing and citation-information with each release .
