national-digital-newspaper-program : a case-study in sharing , linking , and using data this poster presents a case-study describing how the national-digital-newspaper-program 's (ndnp) metadata specification and public website , chronicling america , have been designed to promote a wide range of data-sharing . through use of the website 's extensive application-programming-interface (api) and open-source-software counterpart , several institutions are benefiting from the publicly-funded program 's data .
