using digital-libraries to build educational communities : the chemcollective the chemcollective is a new project in the targeted research track of the national-science-digital-library (nsdl) . the project (http://www chemcollective org) was launched in spring 2004 at the national american chemical society (acs) and national science teachers association (nsta) meetings . the research goal is to explore the degree to which digital-library structures can attract and support a-community of educators working towards a common vision of educational-reform .
