evaluation of header metadata-extraction approaches and tools for scientific pdf-documents this paper evaluates the performance of tools for the extraction of metadata from scientific articles . accurate metadata-extraction is an important task for automating the management of digital libraries . this comparative-study is a guide for developers looking to integrate the most suitable and effective metadata-extraction tool into their software . we shed light on the strengths and weaknesses of seven tools in common use . in our evaluation using papers from the arxiv collection , grobid delivered the best results , followed by mendeley desktop . sciplore xtract , pdfmeat , and svmheaderparse also delivered good results depending on the metadata type to be extracted .
