processing and formatting system for digital-collections this system is being used to build structure-data for the hearth digital-collection and to manage the collection under the dlxs system . it allows student-workers or unskilled employees to build structure metadata from scanned images for both monographs and serials , and manages the process of delivering the titles under dlxs once prepared . it allows supervisors to manage the work , simplifying tasks like re-assigning the in-progress work of graduated students .
