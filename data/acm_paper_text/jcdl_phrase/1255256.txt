locating thematic pinpoints in narrative-texts with short phrases : a test study on don quixote traditional implementations provide only limited assistance for locating the information in narrative-texts relevant to a certain point-of-interest . we are investigating providing a `` reading wheel '' for such purposes . the first step of the bigger picture , as inspired by the editorial compilation of a textbook 's index , is an attempt to locate thematically coherent sentences to a given short phrase . in this paper , we propose a two-step methodology to increase the search-performance and examine its effectiveness in a test study . we describe the experimental setup and report on the quantitative-evaluation of the techniques involved .
