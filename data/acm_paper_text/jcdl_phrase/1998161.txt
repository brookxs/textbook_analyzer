detecting academic-papers on the web our research goal is to develop a search-engine for open-access to academic-papers . english and japanese test-sets were built for detection of academic-papers from 20,000 pdf-files in each language using five annotators . six classifiers were trained using similar features for each language . we report f1 of 0.74 for english and 0.54 for japanese and argue that similar features could easily be generated for other languages as well .
