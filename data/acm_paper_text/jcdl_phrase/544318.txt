the miguel de cervantes-digital-library : a wide diversity of content , media , functionality and services this demo describes the philosophy behind what represents one of the most ambitious projects of its kind in the spanish-speaking world : the miguel de cervantes-digital-library (http://cervantesvirtual.com/) . it shows the new ground being explored in terms of the wide variety of contents , media , functionality and services it offers to a worldwide audience . these services are meant to be used in serious research , as teaching aids , or just for cultural amusement and enjoyment.we will also describe the technical underpinnings of this project reporting also ongoing research-and-development activities .
