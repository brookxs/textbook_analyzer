transcript-free search of audio archives for the national gallery of the spoken word the national gallery of the spoken word (ngsw) project is creating a carefully organized on-line repository of spoken-word collections spanning the 20th century . unprecedented technical challenges are inherent in the development of an archive of such extensive scale and diversity . this paper describes research on the development of text-free search-engine technology used to locate requested content in the audio records . a companion paper in these proceedings addresses watermarking technologies for copyright-protection .
