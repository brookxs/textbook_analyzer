etana-dl : managing complex-information applications -- an archaeology digital library archaeological-research results in the generation of large quantities of heterogeneous-information managed by different projects using custom information-systems . we will demonstrate a prototype digital library (dl) for integrating and managing archaeological data and providing services useful to various user-communities etana -- dl is a model -- based , componentized , extensible , archaeological dl that manages complex-information sources using the client -- server paradigm of the open-archives-initiative protocol for metadata-harvesting (oai -- pmh) .
