inpho : a system for collaboratively populating and extending a dynamic-ontology inpho is a system that combines statistical text processing , information-extraction , human expert feedback , and logic-programming to populate and extend a dynamic-ontology for the field of philosophy . integrated in the editorial workflow of the stanford encyclopedia of philosophy (sep) , it will provide important metadata features such as automated-generation of cross-references , semantic-search , and ontology driven conceptual-navigation .
