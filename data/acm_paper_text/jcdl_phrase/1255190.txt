the oai-ore effort : progress , challenges , synergies the panel will discuss various aspects of the ongoing object re-use and exchange (ore) effort of the open-archives-initiative (oai) . oai-ore is funded by the andrew w. mellon foundation and is a result of the `` augmenting interoperability across scholarly-repositories '' meeting that took place in april 2006 at the mellon foundation . a panel at jcdl 2006 reported on this meeting . the goal of oai-ore is to develop , identify , and profile extensible standards and protocols that allow repositories , agents , and services to interoperate in the context-of-use and reuse of compound digital-objects beyond the boundaries of the holding repositories .
