semantic alerting for digital-libraries we previously investigated the support of alerting services across networks of heterogeneous digital-libraries . we now report the first generation of semantically enhanced digital-library alerting-systems . where previous alerting services have provided users with notifications of new library content using traditional metadata , we demonstrate the advantages and challenges of using semantic-technologies . this uncovers key issues that are not yet fully understood in general event-based-systems (including alerting-systems) .
