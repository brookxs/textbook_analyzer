the digital library of historical-cartography of the university of s o paulo the digital library of historical-cartography of the university of s o paulo makes available a set of high-resolution digital versions of maps printed between the xv and the xix centuries belonging to the university 's collections . each map is available along with extensive carto-bibliographic and biographic references , and relevant technical , editorial and historical-information for cartographic documents analysis . the digital library was also conceived to pursue data from other similar sites , constituting itself as a useful research-tool . it provides facilities to gather relevant-information to acknowledge the production , circulation and appropriation of historical-maps in different contexts and media .
