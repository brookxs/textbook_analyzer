driade : a data-repository for evolutionary-biology nescent (the national evolutionary synthesis center) is developing driade (digital-repository of information and data for evolution) to address synthetic research-challenges fundamental to advancing the field of evolutionary-biology . this poster highlights results from a survey of selected repositories ' functionalities , driade 's functional-requirements , and driade 's functional-model . we also summarize ongoing research activities , studying evolutionary biologists ' data-preservation practices and use requirements .
