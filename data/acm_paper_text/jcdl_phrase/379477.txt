technology and values : lessons from central-and-eastern-europe technology does not develop independently of its social-context . rathe r , there is a range of social , cultural and economic-factors (in addition to technical factors) that define the parameters for the development and use of technologies . this paper presents a case-study of the social shaping of one aspect of digital-libraries , the development of national-union-catalogs (nuc) , in four countries of central-and-eastern-europe (cee) . it examines the specific choices and values that are embedded in the design of a nuc , and how these might be transferred to other cultural contexts .
