emu : the emory user-behavior-data management-system for automatic library-search-evaluation we describe emu , a system for collecting , managing , and mining the behavior data collected in the emory libraries search-system . we describe the data-capture system based on the libx browser-plugin , the database-management-system for successfully storing , searching and exploring millions of resulting user-interactions , and preliminary results of interesting queries and statistics that we are using to evaluate the effectiveness of library search tools .
