airframe : integrating diverse digital-collections in astrobiology astrobiology is an inherently interdisciplinary field concerned with questions of life in the universe . this paper describes the design and ongoing implementation of the astrobiology integrative-research framework (airframe) , an open-source , ontology-driven information-system designed to ingest and analyze heterogeneous inputs of both published and unpublished data , and to identify and illustrate latent connections between research in astrobiology 's diverse constituent fields .
