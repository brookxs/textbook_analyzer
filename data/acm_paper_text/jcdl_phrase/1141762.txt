also by the same author : aktiveauthor , a citation-graph approach to name-disambiguation the desire for definitive data and the-semantic-web drive for inference over heterogeneous-data-sources requires co-reference-resolution to be performed on those data . in particular , name-disambiguation is required to allow accurate publication lists , citation counts and impact measures to be determined . this paper describes a graph-based-approach to author-disambiguation on large-scale citation-networks . using self-citation , co-authorship and document source analyses , aktiveauthor clusters papers , achieving precision of 0.997 and recall of 0.818 over a test group of eight surname clusters .
