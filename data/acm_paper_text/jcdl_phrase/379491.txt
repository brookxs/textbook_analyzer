overview of the virtual-data-center project and software in this paper , we present an overview of the virtual-data-center (vdc) software , an open-source digital-library-system for the management and dissemination of distributed-collections of quantitative-data . (see) . the vdc functionality provides everything necessary to maintain and disseminate an individual collection of research studies , including facilities for the storage , archiving , cataloging , translation , and on-line analysis of a particular collection . moreover , the system provides extensive support for distributed and federated collections including : location-independent naming of objects , distributed-authentication and access-control , federated metadata-harvesting , remote repository caching , and distributed virtual collections of remote objects .
