dynamic online views of meta-indexes for a collection of digitized monographs in a subject domain , a domain meta-index provides a summary of domain-concepts , and a structured vocabulary to support a scholar 's navigation and search . we present a prototype of a meta-index user-interface (mui) that provides views of a domain at three levels : summarizing and comparing domains , exposing the regularities of a domain 's vocabulary , and displaying book information and page content related both to objectively-representative books , and to specific user searches .
