search-engine driven author-disambiguation in scholarly digital libraries , author-disambiguation is an important task that attributes a scholarly-work with specific authors . this is critical when individuals share the same name . we present an approach to this task that analyzes the results of automatically-crafted web-searches . a key observation is that pages from rare web-sites are stronger source of evidence than pages from common web-sites , which we model as inverse host frequency (ihf) . our system is able to achieve an average accuracy of 0.836 .
