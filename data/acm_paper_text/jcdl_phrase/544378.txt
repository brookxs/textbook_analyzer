text-retrieval-conference (trec) genomics pre-track workshop the goal of this workshop is to allow individuals interested in the text-retrieval-conference (trec , trec.nist.gov) genomics pre-track to come together to discuss common goals and interests for the pre-track . the workshop will be designed to generate a plan for developing a common set of tasks , databases , and evaluation-measures for the pre-track . the morning will be devoted to presentations by attendees , with the topics to be covered determined by selection by the program-committee . the afternoon will be geared towards developing a plan for the pre-track , with the structure based on the number of attendees (i.e. , if attendance is large , we will break into small-groups) .
