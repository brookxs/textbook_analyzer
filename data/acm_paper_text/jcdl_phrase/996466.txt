the oai-pmh nasa technical report server the nasa technical report server (ntrs) is now based on the open-archives-initiative protocol for metadata-harvesting (oai-pmh) . this metadata-harvesting version of ntrs represents a significant improvement over the previous distributed-searching implementation of ntrs . in addition to being an oai-pmh service-provider , the new version of ntrs is also an oai-pmh aggregator . this allows ntrs to serve as a one-stop shop for harvesting of nasa metadata .
