how do users ' search tactic selections influence search outputs in exploratory-search tasks ? this study investigates the relationships between users ' search tactic selections and search outputs while conducting exploratory searches in digital-libraries . frequencies of different types of search-tactics applied in an exploratory-search task were counted . based on correlation-analysis and multiple-regression-analysis , we identified types of search-tactics that are associated with aspectual recall . preliminary results indicate that browsing and evaluating item tactics affect aspectual recall in exploratory-search tasks .
