evaluating g-portal for geography learning and teaching this paper describes g-portal , a geospatial digital library of geographical assets , providing an interactive platform to engage students in active manipulation and analysis of information-resources and collaborative-learning activities . using a g-portal application in which students conducted a field-study of an environmental-problem of beach erosion and sea level rise , we describe a pilot-study to evaluate usefulness and usability-issues to support the learning of geographical concepts , and in turn teaching .
