building digital-library-collections with greenstone this tutorial will demonstrate how to build a variety of different kinds of digital-library-collections with the greenstone-digital-library-software , a comprehensive , open-source system for constructing , presenting , and maintaining information collections . collections will be built from html-documents ; word , pdf and postscript-documents ; images in various formats ; mp3 and midi audio ; marc-records ; and more . for each collection , various different full-text-search indexes and metadata-based browsers will be created.attendees who wish to are encouraged to bring their laptops , install greenstone from a cd-rom that we will provide , along with various sample files , and follow along with the demonstrations on their own machine .
