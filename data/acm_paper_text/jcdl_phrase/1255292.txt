what do faculty need and want from digital-libraries ? in this paper , we report on the results of a national survey of faculty members concerning their use of digital-resources (drs) , collections of resources and digital-libraries (dls) . the research reported here explored issues such as : value of drs , motivation for using drs and barriers to use of these resources in teaching . the results have implications for how dls might develop education and outreach efforts to increase visibility and use of their collections .
