xeb : a markup-language-document container-format suitable for handheld-devices we propose a new document container-format (xeb , extensible electronic-book) based on block mechanism to efficiently process markup-language documents in handheld-devices . and random document access is also supported in the format through a pagination mechanism . the format has already been applied to a number of handheld-devices ' chinese e-book readersand xeb documents can be downloaded from a chinese e-book store .
