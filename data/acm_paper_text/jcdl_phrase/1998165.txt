is tagging multilingual ? : a case-study with bibsonomy this paper investigates the occurrence of tags in different languages in a collaborative bookmarking and publication sharing service - bibsonomy . social-tags assigned to urls in multiple languages and users tagging these urls multilingually are the main focus of this study . the results show that multilingual tags occur for the same url and that users tag in different languages . furthermore , the results give indications that the language of the content of a url does not imply that its tags are in the same language .
