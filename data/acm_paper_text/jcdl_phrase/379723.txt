an approach to search for the digital library the chief form of accessing the content of a-digital library (dl) is i ts search-interface . while a dl needs an-interface that integrates a range of options from search to browse to serendipity , in this work we focus on analytical search . we propose using bates ' search-tactics as a basis for the re-design of search-interfaces . we believe this approach will help to identify the types of tools that need to be supported by a dl interface .
