a qualitative-analysis of information-dissemination through twitter in a-digital library this study examines the use of twitter in a-digital library , the-internet public-library (ipl2) , to understand the content and dissemination patterns of twitter-messages posted by the ipl2 . we conducted a content-analysis on ipl2 's messages on twitter to develop a categorization of the type of tweets , and examined retweets and the active users who retweeted ipl2 tweets . we present our analysis of four areas related to the tweets : motivation , content , audience , and sources . active users are categorized into eight groups . the research findings contribute to a further understanding of the actual use of twitter in a-digital library .
