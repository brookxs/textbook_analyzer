visualizing and exploring picasso 's world we discuss the preliminary use of a visualization-tool called interactive-timeline viewer (itlv) in visualizing and exploring a collection of art works by pablo ruiz picasso . our data-set is composed of a subset of the on-line picasso project , a significantly-sized on-line art repository of the renowned spanish artist . we also include a brief discussion about how this visualization-tool can help art scholars to study and analyze an artist 's life and works .
