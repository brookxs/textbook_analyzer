review-mining for music-digital-libraries : phase ii we continue our work on the automatic mining of user-created music-reviews towards the goal of connecting user opinions to music objects in music-digital-libraries (mdl) . we demonstrate an experimental-system which automatically discovered the key descriptive-patterns that differentiated positive from negative reviews which helps us to better understand our successful phase i results . comparison to an earlier study indicates an important consistency across projects that warrants further investigation .
