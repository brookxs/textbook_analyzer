the open-archives-initiative : building a low-barrier interoperability-framework the open-archives-initiative (oai) develops and promotes interoperabil ity solutions that aim to facilitate the efficient dissemination of content . the roots of the oai lie in the e-print community . over the last year its focus has been extended to include all content providers . this paper describes the recent history of the oai - its origins in promoting e-prints , the broadening of its focus , the details of its technical standard for metadata-harvesting , the applications of this standard , and future plans .
