developing a flexible content-model for media repositories : a case-study this article describes the process and challenges of developing a content-model that can support the content and metadata present in a complex media archive . media-archives have some of the most diverse requirements in an effort to catalog , preserve , and make accessible a wide range of content with multifaceted relationships between works . we focus particularly on the design-and-implementation of the wgbh media library and archives ' fedora digital access repository for scholars , educational users and the public . it is our hope that the process and findings from this work can support the architecture and development of other media-archives .
