supporting revisitation with contextual suggestions web-browsers provide only little support for users to revisit pages that they do not visit very often . we developed a browser toolbar that reminds users of visited pages related to the page that they currently viewing . the recommendation-method combines ranking with propagation methods . a user-evaluation shows that on average 22.7 \ % of the revisits were triggered by the toolbar , a considerable change on the participants ' revisitation routines . in this paper we discuss the value of the recommendations and the implications derived from the evaluation .
